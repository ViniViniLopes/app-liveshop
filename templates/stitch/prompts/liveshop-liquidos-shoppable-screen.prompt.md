# Stitch Prompt — LiveShop LiquidOS Shoppable Screen

Create a vertical live commerce screen inspired by Apple Liquid Glass and modern social commerce.

Visual direction:
- full-screen vertical video/live background
- floating glass product card in lower area
- neon lime accent (#A3FF00)
- dynamic translucent glass panels
- rounded squircle corners, very large radius
- soft blur/refraction, subtle glow
- right-side social action rail
- bottom checkout CTA that feels native and premium

States:
- recorded video
- live now
- replay
- product card collapsed
- product card expanded
- checkout processing
- purchase success badge

Do not hard-code product data.
Every CTA must map to a Supabase action event.
